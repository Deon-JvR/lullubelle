Helpful answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear guidance before choosing a treatment or booking your appointment.

How many Microneedling sessions will I need?

The recommended number varies according to your skin, concerns and goals. Luzelle will recommend a personalised treatment plan after consultation.

Does this facial include extractions?

Yes. After carefully analysing your skin, your therapist will determine whether extractions are necessary. If they are required, they are included as part of your facial treatment at no additional charge.

What is Cosmelan?

Cosmelan is a professional depigmentation programme designed to improve the appearance of uneven pigmentation and support a more even-looking skin tone with professional treatment and prescribed home care.

Is Epilfree permanent?

Epilfree is used as part of a progressive hair-reduction programme. Results and the number of sessions required vary between clients, and maintenance may be recommended.

Which facial is best for acne?

The best facial depends on the type and condition of your skin. A consultation allows Lullubelle to select an appropriate clarifying or advanced facial and home-care routine.

How do I book an appointment?

Use the Book Appointment page to choose your treatment, preferred date and time, then send the prepared request through WhatsApp.
