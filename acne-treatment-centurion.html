Acne FAQ

Before booking

Can a facial help acne?

A professional facial may support congestion and breakouts when selected for your skin condition.

Does this facial include extractions?

Yes. After carefully analysing your skin, your therapist will determine whether extractions are necessary. If they are required, they are included as part of your facial treatment at no additional charge.

Will I need products?

Home care is often important for breakout-prone skin and will be recommended carefully.

Is treatment private?

Yes. Lullubelle is an appointment-only studio in Centurion.
