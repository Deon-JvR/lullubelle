Choosing your facial

Which facial is best for acne?

The best option depends on whether your skin is inflamed, congested, sensitive or dehydrated. Luzelle will select a suitable clarifying treatment after assessing your skin.

How often should I book a facial?

Frequency depends on your treatment goals, skin condition and home-care routine.

Can a facial be adapted for sensitive skin?

Yes. Products and treatment intensity can be adjusted after consultation.
